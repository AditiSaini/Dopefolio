Data Analytics and Mining- Global Terrorism Database

Access Project Report

Project Overview

This project analyzes a comprehensive global terrorism database to predict responsible terrorist groups and the success of their attacks. The study spans from 1970 to 2017, employing various classification models, including Decision Tree and XGBoost. Results indicate tree-based models excel in predicting terrorist groups, while XGBoost performs best in predicting attack success. The findings offer valuable insights for in-depth analysis of terrorist capabilities and inform future measures to counteract terrorism.
